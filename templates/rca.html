🧠 How the Smart RCA Works

The system uses keyword matching + frequency analysis against your actual historical tripping data.
When you enter symptoms (e.g. "detonation", "fuel valve", "spark plug"), it:
  1. Matches your keywords against 16 root cause categories learned from this plant's history
  2. Ranks causes by how often they occurred for the selected engine
  3. Retrieves the most similar past incidents for reference
  4. Lets you confirm or add a new root cause to the learning database
